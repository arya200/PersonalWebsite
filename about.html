About me!

I play the drums and love to Scuba Dive. After joining the organisation, ReefWatch Marine Conservation based in the Andaman islands, India, I obtained my Advanced open water scuba diving certification from P.A.D.I and have spent a month on a boat in the Indian Ocean, exploring different dive spots. My most exciting dive encounter would have to be swimming 3 feet away from a tiger shark.

My love for Computer Science started when I took a Java Game Design course in the 9th Grade. Games have not only fulfilled that need to escape but have also brought diverse groups of people together.
